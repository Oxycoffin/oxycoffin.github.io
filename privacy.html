Privacy Policy

Last updated:

At Lagartija Labs, we take privacy seriously. This policy explains what data our apps may collect, why we collect it, and how you can exercise your rights.

Data we collect

  • Anonymous usage data to improve stability and performance.
  • Required data to enable explicit app features.
  • We never sell personal data or collect more than necessary.

Purposes and legal basis

We use data only to:

  • Provide and maintain the service.
  • Detect, prevent, and resolve issues.
  • Improve user experience in aggregate.

Retention

We retain data only as long as needed for the purposes above.

Third parties

Some apps may integrate third-party services (e.g., Google Play Services). Check each app listing for specific details.

Crash/Diagnostics

  • Provider: Sentry.
  • Data: stack traces, app version/build, OS/device info, breadcrumbs, background errors, attached diagnostics.log.
  • Purpose: stability and improvements.
  • Retention: per Sentry's policy.
  • Opt-out: Settings → Privacy → "Share diagnostics".

Your rights

You can request access, correction, or deletion by emailing lagartijalabs@gmail.com.

Contact
