Approach

Useful enough to keep. Distinct enough to recall.

Lagartija Labs leans into the kind of software people actually enjoy revisiting: lighter releases, stronger interaction, and a visual point of view that does not need ten paragraphs to justify itself.

01

Cut the feature cemetery.

Ship the sharp version first. Keep what reduces friction. Remove what only makes the roadmap look impressive.

02

Make privacy part of the experience.

Data restraint should be felt in the product, not buried in the legal footer. Faster flows and less collection usually belong together.

03

Design for memory, not just clarity.

Clean UX matters. So does mood, texture, pacing, and the small decisions that stop a tool from feeling anonymous.
